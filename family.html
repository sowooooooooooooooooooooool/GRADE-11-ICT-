My Family

My Family is very complex. I live with my Mother and Sister. I do not live with my Father. Me and my Sister are not full blood siblings, only half blood. I have other siblings from my Father's side but they are also not my full siblings, only half. I am kind of an only child technically. Although I was raised alongside my Sister from my Mother's.

I don't have much to say about my family. I have cousins, aunts and uncles too. Grandpas and Grandmas as well. Although most have been deceased. I am not particularly close with my family but I can still tolerate being with them. I don't hate them much. I hope I will add on additional family members, like a partner.

I am quite content with the current state my family is in. Though, I hope it will always get better than before. I don't want my family to feel dread in any way. I love them quite a bit. I will always try to be a good kid, despite getting in trouble sometimes. I will make them proud. I am sure of it. I will. For the best of them.
