Introduction

I was born on the 13th of October in the year 2009, I am a libra.

I am a very reclusive person, I quite like being alone as I am often am. I learned most of my knowledge by my own experience, I could call myself independent as a child. It was very much hard for me to actively socialize with peers back then. I wasn't much of a loner but I definitely wasn't anyone's best friend.

People mostly would say I was smart as a child, even one of a kind... though it is not true anymore. I wasn't much of a troublemaker as a kid but I did have my moments. I had a problem with anger as a child, I would lash on others after suppression, I try not to now. What I did as a child was simple, I mainly played games or dwell on the internet, I was very much exposed to technology.

Body

In my teenage years or the years in which I was up and coming to be one, I was confused in my endeavors. As growing into the adolescence stage, It was during the pandemic in which I was always inside the house.

I believe I became more indulged in the absence of companionship, this was the time in which I consumed much media. I didn't have such friends that I could've communicated at that time, though I was still very much in awe on my exploration of what I like. It was the beginning of my self identity, though I haven't fully grasped on it.

This time of life was mainly boring as it would just usually be me doing my own stuff which isn't completely interesting. I was oblivious to the reality of the world, still being happy go lucky as I was. I definitely had much to learn, which would eventually astonish me in ways.

Conclusion

The recent years, the time of both euphoria and sorrow, the time of being immersed in the real world. This was youth, my youth. I believe this was the true finding of what I am, who I am as a person.

8th grade, I was pretty normal, started out quiet but still had enjoyable fun due to me gaining a friend group of five. 9th Grade, the start of the absolute life of mine, I would say. This was the time of curiosity, I gained new friends and especially liked a girl. That girl was someone I knew from elementary, a familiar face. Although, nothing really happened that would make us dependently close, that was about it.

10th Grade, I was in my prime social life, I hung out with a lot people, mostly became somewhat normal as in no particular shyness or distinct from others. Grades were in an all time high, I befriended everyone, with the highest social status friends group in class. Now, currently in 11th Grade, Senior High School. I am finding my purpose or somehow trying to achieve such a thing to go and continue living on.
